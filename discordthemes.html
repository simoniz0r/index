Autohide Mod

A theme that is styled to be as minimal as possible while still looking nice when the window size is small. It uses UndeadLeech's undead-discord theme to allow you to change the color of Discord to your liking just by a few hex colors in the root section of the theme. The guilds wrapper will be hidden to the left, and the channel and member list will be hidden to the right when the window width is below 1000px. After being hidden, both can be activated by hovering your mouse at the edge.
